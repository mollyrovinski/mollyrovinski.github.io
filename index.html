Molly Rovinski

PhD Student in Economics & Hayek Fellow, George Mason University

I'm a PhD student in the Department of Economics at George Mason University and a Hayek Fellow at the F.A. Hayek Program for the Advanced Study of Politics, Philosophy, and Economics at the Mercatus Center.

My research draws on Austrian economics to examine questions of immigration and foreign intervention, exploring how institutional and policy environments shape human movement and international affairs.
